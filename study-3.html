La Via National U

A New Innovative University

A digital publishing company looking for the brand to transmit: trust and stability, while still capturing the eye. The client wanted it to be more conservative than ‘fun’ and ‘edgy’. The client also wanted to exhibit no signs of a book or writing utensil describing that it was a publishing company, but rather something clean and simple.
